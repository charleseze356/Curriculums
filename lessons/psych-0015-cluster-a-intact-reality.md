*This lesson zooms into Cluster A from the personality map. Place each disorder by the reason relationships fail: distrust, indifference, or eccentric fear and cognition.*

## Three pathways to social distance

| Disorder | Core pattern | Key contrast |
|---|---|---|
| Paranoid | Distrust; interprets motives as malevolent | Suspicion without a fixed delusional system |
| Schizoid | Detachment, restricted affect, little desire for relationships | Does not seek closeness; unlike avoidant PD |
| Schizotypal | Eccentric behavior, magical thinking, ideas of reference, unusual perceptions | Odd beliefs without sustained overt psychosis |

*Sources: FA Step 2 CK 8E, Psychiatry p. 403; FA Clinical Algorithms, Ch. 27-3, pp. 779–780.*

## Why “intact reality” matters

Cluster A overlaps phenotypically and genetically with schizophrenia-spectrum illness, but the personality pattern is not itself persistent psychosis. If hallucinations, fixed delusions, marked disorganization, or deterioration dominate, move to the psychosis map rather than forcing a personality diagnosis.
